The Best Movies According to Sam

My top 3 movies of all-time

Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King

The ending to rule them all. No other movie has quite finished a fantasy story as beautifully as this one.

The Green Knight

A deep and introspective take on the classic Arthurian tale. One that will keep you thinking long after the credits roll.

There Will Be Blood

Daniel Day Lewis giving a power house of a performance in this story of how greed can turn us all into monsters.
